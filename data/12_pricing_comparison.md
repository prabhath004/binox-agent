## Cost Per Developer Per Year

Assuming a team of 10 developers:

- Continue: $0 (self-hosted)
- Cody Pro: $1,080/year
- Copilot Individual: $1,200/year
- Tabnine Pro: $1,440/year
- Windsurf Pro: $1,800/year
- Cursor Pro: $2,400/year
- Replit Core: $3,000/year
- Copilot Enterprise: $4,680/year
- Cursor Business: $4,800/year
- Devin Team: $6,000/year

## Value Analysis

Best value for individuals: Cody Pro ($9/mo) or Windsurf Pro ($15/mo)
Best value for teams: Continue (free) or Copilot Business ($19/user/mo)
Best for enterprises with privacy needs: Tabnine Enterprise (on-prem)
Highest cost-to-value risk: Devin ($500/mo for unproven reliability)
